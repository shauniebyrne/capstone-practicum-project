Position Statement

Our gift of agency is something that we learn about in the gospel from when we are young in primary. I have heard the word ‘agency’ many times and recognize it as the gift I have been given from God to choose for myself. I recognized that this gift was so important, even in the pre-existence, that 1/3 of our Heavenly Parent’s children were allowed to be lost. I also recognized why agency is so important. It is the way that we show who we are willing to be and what place we are willing to try to earn after the judgment. Though this gift is a bit daunting and a big responsibility, I am grateful for the chance to prove myself to Him and be able to choose for myself. I recognize the importance of choosing the right and allowing my children to do the same. I will strive to do my best to remember that Heavenly Father has allowed us to choose and make wrong choice, and I need to be able to do the same for my kids; always being there to help them after.
