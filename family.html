Position Statement

Through this study and reading talks about families, I have been able to better appreciate my families; my immediate family, extended family, and ward families. I have been able to relearn their importance in not only my life, but also in the Plan of Happiness. I recognize more firmly that these “family” relationships I am making on earth are eternal. I have a responsibility to do my best to keep my family relationships strong, spiritual, and important. The natural man in all of us tends to take for granted what we should cherish the most. I recognize the importance of making my family relationships my number one priority and doing my best to help them succeed. I am, and will forever be, grateful for all my families and that those relationships will not end when we die. I have been blessed with a forever family.
