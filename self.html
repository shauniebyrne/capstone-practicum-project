Position Statement

I have learned a recurring theme in my study of this topic, that it is a commandment to love ourselves. This includes forgiving ourselves as well. I have been to a few self-reliance classes, and we are taught this important truth, we can’t share with others if we have nothing. I feel this truth is relatable to self-love and self-forgiveness as well. We can’t fully love others or forgive others if we are not full ourselves. I struggle greatly with this concept. I have no self-love and hold onto guilt, even after repentance. I have come to realize that I am not being fair to myself or to my loved ones. I have learned so much from reading these conference talks. I hope to take what I have learned and to change, to love and forgive myself. I want to have strong, loving relationships and I recognize that the first step to achieving this is to love and forgive myself.
