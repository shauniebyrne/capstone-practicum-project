Position Statement

I have been so impressed with the many talks available, literally at my fingertips, that give great parenting advice. Let’s be honest, I need each and every one of them. I am guessing with each turn and just hoping for the best. Thank goodness I have the gospel, prayer, and revelation to help with the guess work of parenting. The weight of responsibility is immense, with trying to raise good children in the gospel and the community and to keep them happy and alive. The messages of advice and help were so comforting to me. I learned things I need to do better and things that will bring success to my family. I have learned the importance I set by my example. I have also learned that the greatest thing I could do for my children is just love them and try to treat them as Heavenly Father or the Savior would. When I can accomplish that, I have achieved parenthood.
